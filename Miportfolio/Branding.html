Rebranding Sumo

Sumo is a Japanese fast-food franchise located in major cities across Spain. With this redesign, my goal was to preserve Sumo’s core values—freshness, quality, and approachability—while creating a design that better fits the brand’s current needs and communicates its message more clearly.

I chose to design a logo that is simple but playful. I used a bold black typeface and highlighted the letter M in red to make it stand out. To include an illustration that felt naturally integrated with the name, I modified the M to look flattened and placed a sumo wrestler on top of it.
